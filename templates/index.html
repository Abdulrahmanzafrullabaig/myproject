Diabetic Retinopathy Stages

Understanding the progression of diabetic retinopathy

Stage 0 - No DR

No signs of diabetic retinopathy detected. Continue regular monitoring.

Stage 1 - Mild NPDR

Mild non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy with microaneurysms.

Stage 2 - Moderate NPDR

Moderate non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy with hemorrhages and exudates.

Stage 3 - Severe NPDR

Severe non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy with extensive hemorrhages.

Stage 4 - Proliferative DR

Proliferative diabetic retinopathy with neovascularization. Immediate treatment required.
